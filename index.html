LEGO Monkey Kid

PIGSY

  • This guy is the owner of a noodle shop called "Pigsy's Noodles".
  • He took in the MK(the main character) when he was a child and raised him.
  • He fights with a Rake in Season 4 of the show.

MK

  • He is the MC of the series.
  • He wields a golden staff.
  • He is the successor of sun wukong/ wukong's student.

SUN WUKONG

  • He is MK'S Teacher and Idol
  • Although he passed his weapon down to MK, he still has a lot of superpowers and abilities.
  • This guy is based of the Sun Wukong from the book series "The Journey To The West"
